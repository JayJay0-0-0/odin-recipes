Best Caesar Salad

Restaurant quality dining in the comfort of your own home!

This easy homeade caesar salad dressing is the perfect side or main dish

Sereve with your choice of greens and vegetables to make it your own.

Ingedients

  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup half-and-half
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Worchestershire sauce

Directions

  1. Combine anchovy fillets with garlic in a food processor, pulse until it's a paste. Process mayonnaise, Parmesan, half-and-half, lemon juice, dijon mustard, and worchestershire sauce with mixture until dressing is creamy. Refrigerate at least 1 hour befor serving
  2. After dressing is chilled, chop and toss your choice of greens and veggies. Drizzle dressing generously. If desired, top with croutons and parmesan cheese
