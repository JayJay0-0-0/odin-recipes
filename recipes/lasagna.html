World's Best Lasagna

Introducing the best damn lasagna in the world! Quick, easy, and cheap...what more is there to want.

Not a natural born cook? No worries, I got you.

Follow the steps and ingredients(exactly) and you won't screw up family dinner and make momma proud.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6.5oz cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 6oz cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teapsoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 12oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4lbs mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Paramesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until browned
  2. Stir in crushed tomato,tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley,basil,saltitalian seasoning,fennelseeds, and pepper. Simmer for 1 1/2 hrs, stir occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8-10minutes. Drain
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta chesese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons of parsely
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  6. Assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over sauce, overlapping slightly.Spread with 1/2 of the cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 mozzarella. Spoon 1 1/2 meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, spray with cooking oil or make sure the foil does'nt touch the cheese
  8. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for additional 25 minutes
  9. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
