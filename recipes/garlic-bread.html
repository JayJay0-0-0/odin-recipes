Roasted Garlic Bread

The perfect side to any dish

Made with a rich parmesan, butter, and garlic spread topped on traditional italian bread

Ingredients

  • 3 heads of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of olive olive
  • 1lbs loaf Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup pre-softened butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Slice off the tops of garlic heads, so the tip of each clove is exposed. Place garlic heads, cut side up, on a baking sheet, drizzled with olive oil
  3. Bake until garlic is softened, around 30 minutes
  4. Set oven to broil
  5. Slice bread loaf in half horizontally
  6. remove garlic cloves from skin in medium sized bowl. Stir in butter, Parmesan, and parsley until well mixed. Spread evenly o both halves of bread
  7. Broil bread until toasted, around 5 minutes
